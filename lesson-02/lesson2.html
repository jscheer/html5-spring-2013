About

Initially hired for interior design and some secondary design work, Thad had no idea this project would take over the next six plus years and every ounce of his creative energy.

The unfortunate passing of well-respected San Francisco architect Paul Holt at the young age of 48 brought Thad the opportunity to take on this dream project in the early design stages.

This 11,448 sq. ft. modern home sits atop or within an 800 acre mountain parcel. The integration of the dwelling into the mountain landscape was a pleasure but also required by strict County building regulations.
